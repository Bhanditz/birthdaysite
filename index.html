March 18th - 27th

Celebrate Docker's Second Birthday!

 

Join with Docker, Go and the Oceanic Society as we celebrate Docker's 2nd birthday with a global open-source-a-thon where Docker core team members and Go community members will teach and mentor people in how to contribute to open source. Contributions include code, documentation, tutorials, videos, and mentoring. Each contribution to the Docker Project will also support the Oceanic Society and its mission to conserve oceans.

Sign up to stay informed as we unveil more information about this worldwide celebration.
